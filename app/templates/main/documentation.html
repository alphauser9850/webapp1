Lab Access Guide

Accessing Your Lab Environment

  1. Login to Your Account

    Use your email and password to access your dashboard

  2. Select Lab Category

    Choose from CCNA, CCNP, or CCIE lab environments

  3. Start Lab Session

    Click "Start Session" on your chosen lab environment

  4. Connect via Web Interface

    Access your lab through our secure web-based console

Note: Labs are available during our operating hours: 6 AM IST to 1 AM PST.
